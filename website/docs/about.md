# About Maimoon ✨

I am a **Data Science and Agentic AI Engineer** based in the UAE, specializing in the intersection of autonomous systems and production-ready intelligence.

My philosophy centers on the idea of the **"Continuous Builder."** I believe that the most powerful AI solutions are those that are not just theoretically sound but are built with the scalability of DevOps and the precision of Data Science.

### **Professional Narrative** 🧭

My journey began with a fascination for how machines learn and reason. This curiosity evolved into a career dedicated to building systems that *think* and *act* autonomously. From mastering the fundamentals of Python to deploying complex multi-agent architectures using **CrewAI** and **LangGraph**, I have consistently focused on high-impact solutions.

**DevOps** has been the silent engine of my progress. By integrating Docker, Kubernetes, and CI/CD into my AI workflows, I ensure that every agent I build is reliable, scalable, and ready for the real world.

### **What Drives Me** 🚀

*   **Autonomy:** I am passionate about moving beyond simple chatbots to truly agentic systems that can research, execute, and refine their own workflows.
*   **Precision:** Whether it's fine-tuning a RAG pipeline or predicting customer churn, I strive for data-driven accuracy.
*   **Impact:** I build tools that solve real problems—from enhancing educational experiences with AI to automating social media for technical growth.

### **Technical Toolkit** 🛠️

*   **Data Science:** Python, Pandas, Scikit-learn, Vector Databases (Chroma, Pinecone).
