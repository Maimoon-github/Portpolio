# Portfolio Overview 📂

My work is centered on building systems that are not just intelligent, but autonomous and reliable. I focus on three primary pillars:

### **1. Agentic AI & Automation**
Developing multi-agent systems that can research, plan, and execute complex workflows. These agents are designed to reduce human intervention and increase efficiency in tasks ranging from social media management to technical research.

### **2. Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)**
Building high-precision information retrieval systems that anchor Large Language Models (LLMs) in real-world, verified data. This ensures accuracy and context in AI-driven applications.

### **3. Data-Driven Insights**
Leveraging traditional Data Science and Machine Learning to uncover patterns, predict behaviors, and drive strategic decision-making.
