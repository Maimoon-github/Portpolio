## **Agentic AI & Orchestration**

### **Agent-Researcher**
A high-efficiency autonomous research system utilizing **15 specialized processing nodes** to gather, filter, and synthesize comprehensive technical documents.  
*   **Tech Stack:** Python, LangGraph, LangChain, Multi-Agent Nodes.
*   **Impact:** Significantly reduces the time required for deep-dive technical research by automating data synthesis.

### **Social-Media-AI-Agent**
An autonomous content creator that analyzes technical profiles to generate platform-optimized posts for LinkedIn and X (Twitter).  
*   **Tech Stack:** Python, CrewAI, LangGraph, LangChain, Streamlit.
*   **Impact:** Enables consistent, high-quality technical presence with minimal manual overhead.

### **Blog-Writing-Agent**
A sophisticated multi-agent assistant that automates the entire content creation lifecycle, from deep research to relevant image generation.  
*   **Tech Stack:** Python, Streamlit, Ollama (Mistral), SearxNG, Stable Diffusion.
*   **Impact:** Streamlines professional blogging by integrating research and creative assets into a single workflow.

### **instaAutoAI**
A local-first multi-agent pipeline for Instagram automation, creating complete post packages (captions, reels, hashtags) with zero cloud dependencies.  
*   **Tech Stack:** Python, Local LLMs (Ollama), Multi-agent Orchestration.
*   **Impact:** Demonstrates the power of private, local AI for social media management.
